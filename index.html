THE FATE OF FURIOUS (2017)

6.7 | 2 h 16 min | Action , Crime , Thriller

The Fate of the Furious (alternatively known as Fast & Furious 8 and Fast 8, and often stylized as F8) is a 2017 American action film directed by F. Gary Gray and written by Chris Morgan.
